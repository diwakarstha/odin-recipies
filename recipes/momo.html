MOMO Recipe

Description

Momo is most popular nepali culture food. There are varities of momos and they all have their own unique taste

Ingredients

  • flour
  • water
  • onion
  • cabbage
  • meat

Steps

  1. Add water to flour and mix until it forms dough
  2. Make small pieces of dough and roll it in rolling board
  3. Grind the meat using meat grinder
  4. Cut onion and cabbage in small pieces
  5. Mix onion, cabbage and meat together
  6. Pack the mixture of onion, cabbage and meat to rolled dough
  7. Place packed dough in steamer and steam for 15 minutes
